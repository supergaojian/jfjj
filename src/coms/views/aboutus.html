助力中国竞技滑雪接轨国际顶尖水平

目前，中国竞技滑雪亦如热爱它的那群人一般，朝气蓬勃却经验不足，富有激情却难以释放，渴望成长却 时常迷茫，正如无数热爱竞技滑雪的年轻人一般，中国竞技滑雪可谓年轻，充满无限可能。

Currently, just as the zealous young racers shredding on the slopes, Chinese Ski Racing is potent yet unexperienced; vibrant yet constraint; growing yet easily diverted. Just as the speeding figures of youth, it is saturated with possibility.

我们的任务是连接中国和世界。 尖峰竞技在普及竞技知识同时，将致力于引进奥地利，新西兰，美国等国外竞技滑雪的领先技术，并通过世界顶级资源构建专业的培训体系，面向中国青少年。 我们会打造即专业又富有凝聚力的学习平台，为中国竞技滑雪发展贡献一份力量。

The core missions of Tripeak Ski Racing is to establish links between international ski racing and the domestic of China. While spreading knowledge, we hope to introduce top notch race training techniques from Austria, New Zealand and the US to the young Chinese Racers. By doing so, Tripeak Ski Racing wishes to create a professional yet bonding platform; we wish to contribute to Chinese Ski Racing with all that we have.
